Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about getting started, finding gigs, or leveling up your hustle? We’ve got you covered.

How can I find part-time or online jobs through Nia Nairobi?

You can browse our curated listings for part-time, freelance, and online jobs tailored to Kenyan youth. We update them regularly with legit opportunities.

Do I need experience to apply for the jobs listed?

No! Many of our opportunities are beginner-friendly and perfect for students or recent school leavers. We also offer resources to help you build skills.

What kind of skills can I learn through Nia Nairobi?

We offer guidance on digital skills, personal finance, entrepreneurship, creative hustles, and more—everything you need to earn smarter in today’s economy.

Is Nia Nairobi free to use?

Yes, our platform is free to access. Some external learning programs or services we link to may have optional paid upgrades, but most are free.

Can I connect with other young people on the same journey?

Definitely! Our community is designed to help you connect, share tips, and stay motivated with others who are building their hustle and future too.

Is it safe to apply for jobs through Nia Nairobi?

We carefully vet opportunities to ensure they're legit and safe. Still, we encourage you to follow our job safety tips and never share personal info unless you're sure about the source.

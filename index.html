Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, therapy, and mental well-being.

How do I book a therapy session?

You can book a session by visiting our booking page and selecting an available time slot.

Are online sessions available?

Yes, we offer both virtual and in-person sessions based on your preference.

What's the difference between therapy and coaching?

Therapy focuses on emotional healing and mental health, while coaching supports personal development, goal setting, and lifestyle changes.

Can I switch Virtual Coaches if I don’t feel the right connection?

Absolutely! We understand that finding the right Virtual Coach is important, and you can change therapists anytime.

Do I need a subscription to access services?

No, we offer flexible options. You can book individual sessions or choose a monthly plan for ongoing support.

Is my information and session history kept confidential?

Yes, your privacy is our priority. All sessions and personal data are securely protected in accordance with our privacy policy.
